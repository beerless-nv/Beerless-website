Can you imagine one hub that has all the detailed information about beers and breweries? We neither and that's one of the reasons why we want to do it together with you.

At Beerless we think it's important to provide the community a lot, but also accurate information. That's why it is of the utter most importance that every beer has a fully fledged profile. Which contains not only technical details, like alcohol percentage , but also a taste profile and description.

This is something we can't do by ourselves, not even if we wanted to. That's why we need YOUR help!

At Beerless we think it is important that you can easily access all the information you want, that is why we created the Beerless Chatbot. A chatbot you can ask anything related to beers or breweries.

For example if you want to discover some new beers like Westvleteren. You can simply ask our chatbot and he will return you some beers with a similar taste profile.

At Beerless, we think it's important that a beer geek is up-to-date with the latest beer news.

Like everything else, we want to accomplish this with our community. Everyone can write articles or make blogposts that are related to beer.
